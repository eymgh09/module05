MY WORK
Photography is the art of capturing images by recording light, time, and physical form, my photography is based on scenes from my daily life, and mostly from my travels. I love to capture the seasonal changing of the landscape, and nature's elements, and I like to record the energy and atmosphere that is visually appealing, and sometimes photography is just a tool to capture a memory.
