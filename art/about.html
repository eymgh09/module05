My name is Elver Yarin Moscoso, I am a artist, graphic designer, and web developer, who love's Photography, I like how objects physically take shape through the projection of light and shadow, and how this phenomenon can be captured thanks to a photographic shot, it is an optical pleasure that I often enjoy doing. I also use photography as a means to capture images that are relevant to my art, through which I use to orient myself in my pictorial process. photography is more like a tool, since only the mental retention of an image is not necessary to retain the precise shapes and colors of whatever the subject matter
